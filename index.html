FAQ

Everything moderators want to know.

What is WhatMod?

WhatMod is a Chrome extension built for Whatnot moderators. It helps organize saved messages, notes, reminders, shoe details, and dashboard tools in one customizable workspace.

Is WhatMod free?

Yes. WhatMod for Chrome is currently planned as a free extension with no license, subscription, or paid account required.

Do I need Windows or Mac downloads?

No. WhatMod now runs as a Chrome extension, so there are no separate Windows or Mac desktop downloads.

Does WhatMod collect my data?

WhatMod is designed to store your saved messages, notes, reminders, and preferences locally in your browser. It does not require an account or subscription.

Who is WhatMod made for?

WhatMod was designed for Whatnot moderators who want faster access to common messages, cleaner notes, reusable show information, and a more organized moderation workflow.

Is WhatMod affiliated with Whatnot?

No. WhatMod is an independent moderation tool created for moderators who work within the Whatnot ecosystem.
