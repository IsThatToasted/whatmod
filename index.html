FAQ

Everything moderators want to know.

What is WhatMod?

WhatMod is a moderation toolkit built specifically for Whatnot moderators. It helps mods respond faster, stay organized during live shows, manage giveaways, store reusable messages, and keep stream workflows clean and professional.

Who is WhatMod made for?

WhatMod was designed for active Whatnot moderators who handle busy live chats, giveaways, sizing questions, announcements, and repetitive responses throughout streams.

How does WhatMod help during live shows?

Instead of switching between notes apps, documents, or copied text files, WhatMod keeps everything inside one fast-access moderation workspace. Mods can instantly send prepared responses, organize notes, and manage stream workflows more efficiently.

What features are included?

WhatMod includes quick message banks, giveaway tools, editable cards, custom hotkeys, notes management, reusable workflows, persistent saves, and moderator-focused organization tools built specifically for live selling environments.

Do I need technical experience to use it?

No. WhatMod is designed to be simple and fast to use even during high-speed live streams. Most moderators can start using it immediately after setup.

Will new features be added?

Yes. WhatMod is actively evolving with planned improvements including smarter moderation workflows, automation tools, overlays, and expanded moderator utilities.

How does access work?

Access is provided through a 30-day subscription. Once subscribed, you'll receive access to the latest WhatMod build and future updates during your active period.

Is WhatMod affiliated with Whatnot?

No. WhatMod is an independent moderation tool created for moderators who work within the Whatnot ecosystem.
