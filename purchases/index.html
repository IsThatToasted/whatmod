How to use the dashboard

This tool lets you analyze your Whatnot purchase history using the official CSV export from your account.
Step 1 — Download your Whatnot purchases CSV
Open the official Whatnot export page and download your orders CSV file.
Download Purchase CSV
Step 2 — Upload your CSV
Use the CSV File uploader below to load your purchase history into the dashboard.
Step 3 — Explore your data
Filter by seller, search purchases, sort totals, review grouped orders, and analyze charts showing spending trends and seller breakdowns.
Step 4 — Export or edit
Double-click cells to edit entries locally in your browser. Export filtered CSVs, edited CSVs, JSON backups, or chart images anytime.
